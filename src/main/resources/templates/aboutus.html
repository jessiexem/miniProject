Welcome to Vocablify

Vocablify aims to help adult language learners improve their English vocabulary.

How it works


Dictionary

Use the dictionary feature to get word definitions, synonyms and antonyms, rhymes, audio pronunciation and example sentences.

Vocabulary Quiz

Increasing your command of vocabulary can be time-consuming and difficult. Vocablify's vocabulary quiz is an efficient way to radically expand your vocabulary! It can be easily customised to your level, and will save you plenty of time and energy by helping you practice using your existing vocabulary and learn new English words.

This is especially useful when it comes to preparing for exams such as:

  • TOEIC : an international standardized test of English language proficiency for non-native speakers
  • GRE : a standardized test widely taken by Prospective applicants from around the world who are interested in pursuing a master's, specialized master's in business, MBA, JD or doctoral degree
  • IELTS : an English language test for people looking to work, live or study in an English-speaking country
  • TEPS : an English proficiency test developed by the Language Education Institute at Seoul National University based on years of research and more than 50 years’ experience in assessing language proficiency
